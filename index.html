Who We Are

We are a legally registered farmers’ cooperative operating in Ayel Village, Alebere Parish, Itek Sub-County Lira district Northern Uganda, Uganda, bringing farmers together to access better markets, quality inputs, training, and financial opportunities.

Produce Aggregation

Access to certified seeds, fertilizers, and modern farm tools.
