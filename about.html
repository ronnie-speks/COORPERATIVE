About Our Cooperative

Learn more about our mission, vision, history, and leadership guiding our farmers’ success.

Our History

Pur En Lonyo was established in 2020 to unite farmers in Lira District Itek Sub-County for better access to markets, farming resources, and training. Over the years, we have grown to 96 members, achieving sustainable agricultural growth and improved livelihoods.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with resources, knowledge, and market access for sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading farmers’ cooperative in Lira District recognized for innovation, unity, and quality produce.
