About Our Cooperative

Learn more about our mission, vision, specific goals, Core Values, objectives, history and leadership guiding our farmers’ success.

Our History

Itek Sub-County Pur En Lonyo Oil Seed Growers Cooperative society limited was established in 2020 to unite farmers in Lira District Itek Sub-County for better access to markets, farming resources and training. Over the years we have grown to 850 members with 394 Male and 456 Female comprising of 495 Youth between 18yrs - 35yrs achieving sustainable agricultural growth and improved livelihoods.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable agricultural production of Sunflower, Soyabean, Maize value addition, Financial service access, Market access, partnership building and modern technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading cooperative in Lira District providing commercially viable agro-production technologies for youth and their families.

Specific Goals

To improve the communities incomes from modern agriculture and eradicate poverty.

Core Values

  1. Self-Help
  2. Self-responsibility
  3. Democracy
  4. Equality
  5. Solidarity
  6. Honesty
  7. Openness
  8. Accountability and Transparency
  9. Social responsibility and care for others

Objectives

  1. Adopt climate smart crop production technologies.
  2. Improve farming technologies through extension and digital marketing.
  3. Establish agro-input shop for affordable inputs.
  4. Create employment among youth and women.
